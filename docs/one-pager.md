# Meritia — Hire for AI Fluency, Not Against It

*One-page pitch. Customer-facing draft.*

**The problem.** Your firm has rolled out Copilot, ChatGPT Enterprise, or Claude for Work. You've told everyone to "use AI." But when you're hiring a finance analyst, consultant, PM, or associate, you still cannot answer the one question that actually matters: *will this person use AI well?*

Traditional assessments sidestep the problem. Case interviews ban AI. Take-homes can't police it. Psychometrics don't measure it. You're hiring for 2026 with tools built for 2019.

**The product.** Meritia is a realistic workplace simulation that gives every candidate the same AI tools your team uses — a research sidekick, an email inbox with AI drafting, a persona colleague on chat — and measures two things at once:

1. **The output** — memos, emails, decisions — marked against a role-specific rubric by your team or ours.
2. **How they used AI to get there** — prompt quality, verification of AI claims, appropriate delegation, pushback on bad suggestions, edit discipline on AI drafts.

Every AI interaction is captured. Every paste, every tab switch, every response time is logged without touching keystrokes or pasted content. Marking is blind by default — candidates appear as "Candidate A", "Candidate AD" — so the signal your hiring managers see is the work, not the name.

**Why it works.**

- **Realistic.** Candidates do the job the way it's actually done — with AI, under time pressure, with incomplete information.
- **Defensible.** Full audit trail, blind marking, and structured rubrics that hold up in hiring-manager debriefs and in DEI reviews.
- **Fast to deploy.** Role-specific scenario packs for finance, consulting, legal, and product. Live in your pipeline in under two weeks.
- **Cost-controlled.** Prompt caching keeps Claude costs to pennies per candidate; pricing is fixed per seat.

**Who it's for.** Talent leaders at 500–10,000-person firms running mid-to-senior hiring in judgment-heavy roles. If you've already paid for AI tooling and you're hiring people who'll use it daily, you're our customer.

**What comes next.** A 20-minute conversation, a sample assessment you can take yourself, and a pilot cohort in one hiring line at fixed price. No platform fee until you've seen the marker reports.

**Meritia. The benchmark for AI-fluent hiring.**
